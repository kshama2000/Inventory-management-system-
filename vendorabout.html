SUPERTRONIX

The Supertronix Cables Pvt Ltd Manufactures Jelly filled Telephone cables for use of Telephone Service providers landline network.
The company’s database keeps track of a company’s employees, departments, and projects.
This project deals about the Inventory management system that the company has implemented to manage the raw materials and spare parts for Repair and maintenance, as this being a process industry, the entire business has critical bearing on the availability of raw material and spare parts in right quantity and in time.
The company has identified every single raw material required for the continuous running of the industry as well as spare parts required for repair and maintenance of the machinery and has given a unique part code which is a 8 digit code, which is maintained as the primary key.
The important users of this system belong to various departments like planning, purchase, marketing, Finance, Maintenance, stores Manager and the production, Vendor Management, Testing, Quality and Inspection , Administration, IT team and of course the Top management.
